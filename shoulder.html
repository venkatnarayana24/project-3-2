Front raises

Anterior Deltoid (Front Delt):
The anterior deltoid is a small triangular muscle located at the front of your shoulder. Its primary role is to flex the shoulder and move your arm forward. When you perform front raises, you specifically engage this muscle group. Without a well-developed anterior deltoid, everyday tasks like reaching for items on a shelf or putting on a T-shirt would be challenging 123.
