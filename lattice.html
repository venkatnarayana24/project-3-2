Cable Seated rows

Cable seated rows primarily target the muscles of the upper back, including the latissimus dorsi, rhomboids, and middle trapezius. They also engage the biceps, rear deltoids, and lower back to a lesser extent, providing a comprehensive upper body workout.

Latt Pull down

Lat pulldowns primarily target the latissimus dorsi muscles, commonly known as the lats. Additionally, they engage the muscles of the middle back, including the rhomboids and lower trapezius. The biceps, rear deltoids, and forearm muscles are also activated as secondary muscles during this exercise.
