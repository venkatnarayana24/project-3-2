SQUATS

These are compound exercises that engage multiple muscle groups, primarily targeting the lower body, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves, while also involving the core and upper body for stabilization. This exercise involves bending the knees and hips to lower the body into a seated position and then rising back up to the starting position. Squats can be performed with body weight, barbells, dumbbells, kettlebells, or other resistance equipment. They are highly effective for building lower body strength, enhancing muscle mass, improving functional fitness, and promoting overall athleticism. Proper form and technique are essential to maximize benefits and prevent injury during squats
